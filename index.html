Hi. I'm Iris Delikoura.

I am a PhD researcher at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) exploring the intersection of AI and Education. My work focuses on how AI can impact learning experiences and educational outcomes.

I hold an MPhil in Education from the University of Cambridge and a BA in Politics from the University of Essex.
